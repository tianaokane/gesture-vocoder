BEFORE YOU START

🎧

Wear headphones. This instrument uses your microphone. Without headphones your speakers will feed back into the mic and produce a loud screech.

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Put on headphones, then click Start
  2. Allow microphone and camera permissions
  3. Hum or sing — the synth tracks your pitch automatically
  4. Hold one hand up — move it up and down to change harmony interval
  5. Change finger shapes to switch available intervals and timbre
  6. Raise both hands to activate Charli mode
