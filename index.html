BEFORE YOU START

🎧

Wear headphones. This instrument uses your microphone. Without headphones your speakers will feed back into the mic and produce a loud screech. Earbuds work fine.

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Put on headphones, then click Start
  2. Allow microphone and camera permissions
  3. Hum or sing into the mic — vowels work best
  4. Hold your hand up in front of the webcam
  5. Move your hand up and down to change pitch
  6. Change finger shapes to switch scales and modes
will request microphone + camera access
